On Not Being Bored

Sometimes boredom is not the absence of things to do, but the absence of things that feel meaningful.

We scroll, switch, and search — but the gap is not outside.

Maybe boredom is not something to eliminate, but something to stay with.


有时候，无聊并不是无事可做， 而是缺少意义感。

我们不断滑动、切换、寻找， 但那种空白，并不在外面。

或许，无聊不是需要被消解的东西， 而是可以停留的地方。
